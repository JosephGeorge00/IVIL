STRONGER ROOTS

IViL – IIT for Villages is a student run organization in IIT Madras(IITM). It serves as a discussion forum and action platform for students of IITM to implement their ideas for Rural India.It is an institute body approved by the Student Legislative Council, IITM (SLC). The passion of the student-volunteers drives IViL forward . IViL provides a flexible platform where the volunteers have the freedom to pursue their ideas for rural India.
